# Estonian Drone Map

A specialized interactive map tool designed for drone pilots in Estonia. It integrates data from Maa-amet (Estonian Land Board) and real-time Meteo wind data to help plan safe drone flights.

## Installation on Mobile (Full Screen App)

This tool is built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means you don't need to download it from an app store. You can install it directly from your browser, and it will run as a standalone, native-feeling app without the browser's URL bar or navigation buttons.

### For iPhone (iOS Safari)
1. Open Safari and navigate to the map URL: [https://rkirmann.github.io/est-drone-map/dronemap.html](https://rkirmann.github.io/est-drone-map/dronemap.html)
2. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen (the square icon with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down the menu and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Confirm by tapping **"Add"** in the top right corner.
5. You will now see the Drone Map icon on your home screen. Tap it to launch the app in full screen!

### For Android (Chrome)
1. Open Google Chrome and navigate to the map URL: [https://rkirmann.github.io/est-drone-map/dronemap.html](https://rkirmann.github.io/est-drone-map/dronemap.html)
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** icon (⋮) in the top right corner.
3. Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**.
4. Confirm the prompt by tapping **"Install"** or **"Add"**.
5. The Drone Map icon will appear in your app drawer or home screen. Launch it for the full-screen experience.

## Features
- **Mission Planner:** Draw waypoints and calculate estimated flight times.
- **Wind & Climb Tax:** Automatically factors in head/tail winds (at requested altitudes) and climb heights to give accurate time estimations.
- **nDSM Height Overlays:** See building and tree heights directly on the map.
- **Interactive Infrastructure:** Click on powerlines and buildings to see exact heights and voltage estimates.
- **UTM No-Fly Zones:** Displays Estonian EANS UTM prohibited and restricted zones.
